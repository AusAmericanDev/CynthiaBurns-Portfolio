About Me

Hi, I’m Cynthia Burns — originally from the U.S. and now building a tech career in Australia while raising two amazing kids. I’m learning in public, shipping small projects, and improving one build at a time.

I started coding to create a flexible, creative future — one where I can work remotely, challenge my brain, and still be present for my family. The messy couch setups, the toy dinosaurs next to VS Code — that’s my reality, and I’m not waiting for perfect conditions to grow.

Right now, I’m focused on mastering front-end development with the long-term goal of going full stack. I’m exploring roles, freelancing, and product ideas — discovering where I fit best by building things.

This site will evolve as I do — more projects, more learning, more proof of progress.
